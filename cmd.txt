IMP CMD TO CREATE AND REMOVE DIRECTORY AND FILES

1.mkdir name  (make directory)
2.dir         (list of directory)
3.rmdir name  (permanently remove directory)

1.echo > file.txt (create file)
2.notepad file.txt (create file)
3.del file.txt (permanently delete file)
